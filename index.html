About me

My name is Dannen Roberts and I am a 22 year old new grad. I graduated from The University At Buffalo in Spring 2020 with a 3.9 GPA. I have a BS in Computer Science and a Minor in mathematics. I will be starting as a Software Development Engineer at Amazon in Boston in July 2020.


My interests include all of the cliche CS ones such as video games, movies, and light social interaction. Slightly less cliched, I like rock climbing, occasional bouts of fitness, Death Cab For Cutie, and eating uncooked pasta. I went into computer science because I like, in descending order of importance: Problem Solving, Cool Tech, Collaboration, Money, and not standing.


My goal with this website is to gain a deeper understanding of web development and to create a convenient one stop shop for anyone trying to learn about me. I'm also going to do a blog about nothing in particular. The prospects of me actually updating even remotely frequently are not likely, we'll see, definitely don't get your hopes up.
